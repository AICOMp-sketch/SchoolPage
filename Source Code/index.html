Our Vision & Mission

Founded in 1995, Greenwood Academy has established itself as a leader in progressive education, blending academic rigor with social-emotional learning to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our mission is to cultivate intellectual curiosity, ethical leadership, and global citizenship in every student through a personalized learning journey.

Accredited by the National Association of Independent Schools
100-acre campus with state-of-the-art facilities
Diverse student body representing over 30 countries
Since 1995
